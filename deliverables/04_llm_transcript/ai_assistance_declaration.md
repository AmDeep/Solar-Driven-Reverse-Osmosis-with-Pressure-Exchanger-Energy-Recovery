## Honest assessment of AI's impact

The AI significantly accelerated the *presentation* layer — turning typed equations into formatted code, building the web app, generating SVG drawings, and writing DXF syntax. It probably saved 8–12 hours of formatting and coding work.

The *engineering reasoning* — what to design, what equations apply, what numbers to trust, what to be uncertain about — remained human-directed throughout. The AI made no physics decisions autonomously.

If I had done this without AI assistance, the deliverable would have been a Word document with the same equations and a hand-sketched block diagram. The reasoning content would have been identical; the presentation would have been simpler.
